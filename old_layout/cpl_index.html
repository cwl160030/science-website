About

I am a 5th year graduate student at the University of Oklahoma studying physical/computational chemistry. My general research interests include the modeling of catalytic mechanisms and approaches to improve current simulation methodology. I am particularly interested in the chemistry of metal-organic frameworks, surface catalysis, and homogeneous organometallic chemistry.

For the most up-to-date list of publications, see my Google Scholar page.
