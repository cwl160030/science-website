About

I am a 5th year graduate student at the University of Oklahoma studying physical, computational, and theoretical chemistry. My general research interests include the modeling of catalytic processes and the development of methods that allow for faster or more accurate simulations of chemistry and physics. I am particularly interested in the chemistry of metal organic frameworks and homogeneous organometallic catalysts.
